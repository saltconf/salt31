SALT(ED) Workshop

Workshop on Inclusive Teaching in Semantics

presented by SALTED: the SALT Equity & Diversity committee

May 6, 2021 2-4pm, with optional informal discussion 4-5pm

The day before the main conference formally begins, SALT will hold a two-hour satellite workshop for discussing methods for making teaching in Semantics and Pragmatics more inclusive, with an eye towards welcoming groups currently underrepresented in the subfield. Led by a panel of semanticists and other linguists who have thought deeply about inclusivity in pedagogy, the workshop will concentrate on two areas: (1) making the content of semantics classes more inclusive, including syllabus revisions and example data from a broader range of source languages and language varieties; and (2) making the style of pedagogy more inclusive and welcoming to all students. A portion of the time will be devoted to structured discussions among participants, together and in smaller break-out rooms. The forum will include an optional final hour to facilitate unstructured discussions among attendees.
