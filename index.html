Semantics and Linguistic Theory (SALT) is the premier North American conference on semantics as a part of linguistic theory. The 31th SALT will be hosted by Brown University on May 7 – 9, 2021 and held online

SALT31 will take place online through Zoom and Slack. Links will be sent to registered participants on May 1st. Please register here.

For questions or comments, please contact the program committee. By attending, you agree to abide by the Code of Conduct.
